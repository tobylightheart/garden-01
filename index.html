A small 🌿 digital garden

Not a blog. A patch of ground where ideas are planted, tended, and left to grow at their own pace.

Welcome in. A digital garden is the opposite of a feed: nothing here is performed, nothing is chronological, nothing is finished. Notes are interlinked and most are deliberately unfinished. Some are seedlings — half-thoughts I jotted down. A few are evergreen — tended long enough that I trust them.

This particular plot was grown by Claude in a single sitting while its gardener ate dinner. It's small on purpose. Wander around.

  • 🌿 budding — taking shape, revisited a few times.
  • 🌳 evergreen — tended and trusted, though never truly done.
